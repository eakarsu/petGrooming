1. Start the App

  ./start.sh
  This seeds the database with test data needed for the features to work.

  2. Login

  - Go to http://localhost:3000/auth/login
  - Click "Use Demo Credentials" button - it fills in the form
  - Click Sign In

  3. Test Each Feature

  Groomer Workload (/grooming/workload)

  - Click "Workload" in sidebar
  - Use < > arrows to navigate weeks
  - Click groomer names to filter by individual groomer
  - Click "Today" to return to current week

  Photo Gallery (/gallery)

  - Click "Gallery" in sidebar
  - Use search box to filter by pet/client name
  - Click any photo card to open comparison dialog
  - Stats cards show totals automatically

  Loyalty Dashboard (/loyalty)

  - Click "Loyalty" in sidebar
  - "Loyalty Tiers" tab shows all clients with their tier status
  - Use search to find specific clients
  - "Service Packages" tab shows available packages with savings

  Reminders (/reminders)

  - Click "Reminders" in sidebar
  - "Pending" tab shows pets due for grooming
  - Click "Send Reminder" on any row to generate AI message
  - Review message in dialog, click "Send Reminder" to confirm
  - "Sent History" tab shows previously sent reminders

  Medical Timeline (on Pet Detail)

  - Click "Pets" in sidebar
  - Click any pet row to view details
  - Click "Timeline" tab to see medical history
  - Timeline shows vaccinations, alerts, and notes chronologically
  - Right side shows health summary card

  Health AI Analysis (/health)

  - Click "Health & Safety" in sidebar
  - Click "Health Alerts" tab
  - Click any alert card to open details
  - Click "AI Analyze" button for AI recommendations

  Demo Credentials Button

  - Log out (click Sign Out at bottom of sidebar)
  - On login page, click "Use Demo Credentials"
  - Form auto-fills with admin credentials
